Stuck Job Detection (Full Sync)

Runs every 4 hours. Checks for jobs stuck IN_PROGRESS for >30 minutes across:

  • ebay_sync_log
  • amazon_sync_log
  • bricklink_sync_log
  • brickowl_sync_log
  • amazon_sync_feeds (processing/submitted >30 min old)

Auto-resets stuck jobs to FAILED. Alerts Discord if >3 consecutive zero-progress invocations.
