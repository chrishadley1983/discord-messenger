**City/Region:** General | **Category:** Nature | **Cost Level:** mid | **Source:** @mauka.travels | **Likes:** -1

**Caption:**
> Step by step below 👇

Roadtrip around the Salzburg area in Austria:

Starting point: Salzburg. Spend 1-2 days exploring the city because there’s a lot to see in it.

Then, drive to Liechtensteinklamm, a beautiful gorge located 70km to the south of Salzburg. The gorge is 1.5km long and the highlights of the path are the spiral staircase and the massive waterfall in the end. Liechtensteinklamm Gorge is closed during the winter but it will open back again in the spring.

Next stop: Hallstatt. This fairytale village is one of the most famous spots in the country and even though we found it a bit overcrowded for our taste, it’s still beautiful.

On the next day, go hiking in the Loser Mountain. This place was such a great discovery... you have a beautiful panoramic road and a couple of hiking trails around the area you can pick from. 

You can also visit Loser during the winter, when it works as a ski resort.
