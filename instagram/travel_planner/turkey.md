### Food And Drink in General, Turkey

**City/Region:** General | **Category:** Food And Drink | **Cost Level:** mid | **Source:** @jamesdimitri

**What they said:**
> [Language: en]
I don't know how a cabat man like myself has been sleeping on Tantuni for this long. Ice lamb chicken will be fried on a sack with caught an oil wrapped in lavash bread with tomatoes, parsley and onions. Comes from Mercedes, a self-coast of Turkey, three places for Tantuni in London. One, direct Tantuni, a west green road, just off green lanes. This place has apparently been here for 22 years, serving only one item and doing it very well. The lamb Tantuni are so good and only 450 apart. I had two but could have easily had more. A glass of house made I ran. Two, Niko Tantuni in Enfield. Yup, f***ing Enfield. This family run spot has been going for eight years. Apparently the lady makes them best so we were lucky. I went for the lamb but they also do chicken too, so delicious. And they do other dishes. Like this chickcofter, additionally made with raw meat, Albolga and Kineffa with ice cream. Turkish tea to drink. Three, Mercin Tantuni in Dolston. This place has a five-star rating in Google. Like Niko, they offer more than just Tantuni. But I went for the yogurt loot one Tantuni. This dish consists of two lamb wraps. Sliced up and smothered in yogurt. Then covered in a spicy oil. These look amazing. Another bang over dish. And I went for Turkish tea to drink.
