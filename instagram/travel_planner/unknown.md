### BLUEBERRY BARS

**City/Region:** General | **Category:** Food And Drink | **Cost Level:** budget | **Source:** @yummy_little_belly | **Likes:** 44,978

**Caption:**
> BLUEBERRY BARS!
Ingredients 
* 2 ripe banana, medium 
* Big handful of blueberries 
* 100g porridge oat (1 1/4 us cups)
* 1 big tablespoons smooth peanut butter (or the nut butter you like)

Method 
1. Preheat oven to  180°C (350 degrees F) and lightly grease a baking dish or line it with parchment paper.
2. Smash banana and blueberries, then add nut butter and oat and mix well.
3. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.
4. Allow to cool completely before slicing into bars.
5. Buon appetito!

👶🏼 Suitable from 6 months
❄️ Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days, or freeze for up to 3 months. To defrost, transfer them from the freezer to the refrigerator overnight. You can warm them up in the oven before serving if desired.
