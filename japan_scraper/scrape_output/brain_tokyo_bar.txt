# Japan 2026 — Tokyo Bars (12 places)
Scraped from Reddit + YouTube, March 2026.

- **Baird Beer Taproom** [Harajuku]: Well-regarded craft beer taproom with multiple Tokyo locations.
- **Ginten**: All-you-can-drink bar in Tokyo recommended as a great value option for a night out.
- **Golden Gai** [Shinjuku]: Maze of narrow alleyways with many tiny bars in Shinjuku; most are foreigner-friendly.
- **LA FRANCE**: Cocktail bar in Tokyo featured in a nightlife etiquette guide as a recommended evening drinks spot.
- **LOST Bar** [Shibuya]: Bar in Shibuya recommended among 101 things to do in Tokyo.
- **R261 CIGAR & ROCK** [Shibuya]: A bar visited while staying at Cerulean Tower during a honeymoon trip, noted for its nightlife.
- **SG Low** [Shibuya]: Recommended as a great foreign-friendly introduction to izakaya culture.
- **SPRING VALLEY BREWERY TOKYO**: Craft brewery and taproom in Tokyo, featured among 101 recommended things to do in the city.
- **Tir Na Nog** [Ginza]: Highly recommended for anyone who likes unusual spaces — described as feeling like you've walked into a fairytale.
- **Uokin Brewing** [Shibuya]: Craft beer bar in Shibuya recommended by a local as one of the hidden food and drink spots in the area.
- **Washu Bar Meguri** [Shimbashi]: Sake bar with five starter dishes for 1650 yen paired with sake, standout aged fish sashimi.
- **YEBISU BREWERY TOKYO** [Ebisu]: Yebisu beer's flagship museum and taproom in Ebisu, recommended as one of 101 things to do in Tokyo.
