# Japan 2026 — Tokyo Parks (5 places)
Scraped from Reddit + YouTube, March 2026.

- **Hamarikyu Gardens** [Shiodome]: Recommended alongside Tsukiji Market for a morning in central Tokyo, with a stop at the historic Nakajima Tea House inside.
- **Inokashira Park** [Kichijoji]: Visited en route to the Ghibli Museum, a pleasant park in the Kichijoji area of Tokyo.
- **Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden** [Shinjuku]: Detailed guide advising to use the less-crowded Sendagaya Gate entrance during cherry blossom season and noting the park has vegan menu options.
- **Ueno Park** [Ueno]: A major park in Tokyo, especially recommended during cherry blossom season and for the variety of museums and the Shinobazuno pond benten nearby.
- **Yoyogi Park** [Shibuya]: Recommended as a picnic spot for cherry blossoms in central Tokyo.
