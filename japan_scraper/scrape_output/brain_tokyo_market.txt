# Japan 2026 — Tokyo Markets (5 places)
Scraped from Reddit + YouTube, March 2026.

- **Ameyoko Shopping District** [Ueno]: Recommended for standing bars and yakitori during happy hour, popular with locals.
- **Ameyokocho Market** [Ueno]: A lively outdoor market near Ueno Station recommended as part of the Ueno area exploration, especially for evening street food.
- **Kappabashi Dori** [Asakusa]: A kitchen-equipment shopping street near Asakusa recommended for browsing cookware, utensils, and model food displays.
- **Toyosu Market** [Toyosu]: Recommended as a morning activity on a Tokyo itinerary for fresh seafood and market atmosphere.
- **Tsukiji Outer Market** [Tsukiji]: A famous seafood market recommended for morning visits for fresh fish stalls and sushi restaurants before 2pm closing time.
