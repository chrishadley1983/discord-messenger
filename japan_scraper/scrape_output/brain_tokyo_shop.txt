# Japan 2026 — Tokyo Shops (9 places)
Scraped from Reddit + YouTube, March 2026.

- **Asakusa Unana** [Asakusa]: Food spot in Asakusa featured as part of a local street food tour of the historic neighbourhood.
- **Itoya Ginza** [Ginza]: Large multi-floor stationery shop with a hydroponic garden and cafe.
- **KINJI** [Harajuku]: Cheap vintage/Y2K thrift store near LaForet with good variety and crazy deals.
- **Komiyama Book Store**: Exceptional bookstore with gallery-like floors, vintage movie props, exclusive art sales.
- **LaForet Harajuku** [Harajuku]: Multi-level shopping complex described as the best for Harajuku style.
- **Mitsukoshi** [Ginza]: Department store in Ginza offering a free membership card for 5% off all purchases; visited alongside Ginza 6 and Tsutaya Loft.
- **Nakano Broadway** [Nakano]: A multi-floor shopping complex in Nakano recommended for anime, manga, and pop culture merchandise shopping.
- **Oiwake Dango** [Shinjuku]: Recommended for unusual dango flavours including Strawberry Beanpaste, Mitarashi, and Matcha White Beanpaste.
- **Tako No Narukisoe** [Asakusa]: Asakusa food stall recommended as part of a street food tour of the historic neighbourhood.
