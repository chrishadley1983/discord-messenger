# Japan 2026 — Tokyo Hotels (11 places)
Scraped from Reddit + YouTube, March 2026.

- **Belken Hotel Tokyo**: Highly recommended for having the most comfortable bed of the entire trip, despite a small room.
- **Cerulean Tower** [Shibuya]: A treat hotel stay in Shibuya on an executive floor, described as worth every cent for a honeymoon trip.
- **Hilton Tokyo Odaiba** [Odaiba]: Recommended for a laid-back hotel base despite being a bit out of the way — the reviewer loved crossing the rainbow bridge every morning and found Odaiba relaxed.
- **Hoshinoya Tokyo**: Luxury hotel booked 6 months in advance for a 50% discount.
- **Nohga Hotel Akihabara Tokyo** [Akihabara]: Used as base during a Tokyo/Kyoto trip, mentioned with overall good impressions.
- **Nohga Hotel Ueno** [Ueno]: Rated 9/10, used as a base for nights in Tokyo during a 2-week Japan food-focused trip.
- **Remm Akihabara** [Akihabara]: Praised for a great view of the surrounding city and a massage chair, serving as the first-night hotel during a solo Japan trip.
- **Richmond Premier International Hotel Asakusa** [Asakusa]: Connected to a Uniqlo, described as a good base for exploring Tokyo with easy train access to the city.
- **Sotetsu Fresa Inn Tokyo-Kyobashi** [Kyobashi]: Praised for its convenient location with several metro stops within 5 minutes walking distance, loved by the reviewers.
- **Tokyu Stay Tsukiji** [Tsukiji]: Enjoyed for its convenient location, AC, and in-room washer/dryer — making it ideal for light packing on a 2-week trip.
- **Wise Owl Hostels Azumabashi** [Azumabashi]: Used as base for a solo trip with close proximity to Senso-ji Temple and nearby attractions.
